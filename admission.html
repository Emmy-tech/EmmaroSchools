Admissions

We welcome students from diverse backgrounds who are ready to excel academically and morally.

Admission Process

  1. Purchase admission form at the school office or download below.
  2. Submit completed form with required documents.
  3. Take the entrance examination.
  4. Attend an interview (for parents/guardians).
  5. Successful candidates will receive an admission letter.
